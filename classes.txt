Class: Boat
Attributes:
color (string)
owner (string)
max_number_of_passengers (integer)
number_of_passengers (integer)
enough_crew_members (boolean)
Methods:
paint_job (changes color)
sell_boat (changes owner)
add_passenger (adds one to number_of_passengers)
is_overcapacity (is number_of_passengers greater than max_number_of_passengers)
is_crewed (checks if crew is true or false)
